1. Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken Ingredients:

1 whole chicken (approx. 1.5kg)

3 tablespoons olive oil

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 lemon, halved

1 teaspoon dried oregano

Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps to Cook:

Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).

Rub the chicken with olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.

Place lemon halves inside the chicken cavity.

Roast in a baking dish for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 75°C.

Let rest for 10 minutes before carving.

Nutritional Value (per serving):

Calories: 350 kcal

Protein: 32g

Fat: 22g

Carbohydrates: 2g
