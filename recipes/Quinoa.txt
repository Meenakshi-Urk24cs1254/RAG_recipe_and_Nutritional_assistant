2. Quinoa and Black Bean Salad
Ingredients:

1 cup dry quinoa

1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed

1 red bell pepper, diced

1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

2 tablespoons lime juice

1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps to Cook:

Rinse quinoa and boil in 2 cups of water for 15 minutes until fluffy.

In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, black beans, and bell pepper.

Whisk lime juice and olive oil together; pour over the salad.

Toss with cilantro and serve chilled.

Nutritional Value (per serving):

Calories: 210 kcal

Protein: 9g

Fat: 5g

Carbohydrates: 34g
