4. Pan-Seared Salmon with Asparagus
Ingredients:

2 salmon fillets (150g each)

1 bunch asparagus, woody ends trimmed

1 tablespoon butter

1 teaspoon paprika

Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps to Cook:

Season salmon with paprika, salt, and pepper.

Heat butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.

Sear salmon skin-side down for 4 minutes, then flip and cook for 3 minutes.

Add asparagus to the same pan for the last 5 minutes of cooking.

Nutritional Value (per serving):

Calories: 320 kcal

Protein: 34g

Fat: 18g

Carbohydrates: 4g
