5. Overnight Oats with Berries
Ingredients:

1/2 cup rolled oats

1/2 cup almond milk

1/4 cup Greek yogurt

1/2 cup mixed berries (blueberries/strawberries)

1 teaspoon honey

Steps to Cook:

Combine oats, almond milk, and yogurt in a glass jar.

Stir in honey and layer the berries on top.

Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight (at least 6 hours).

Stir and eat cold in the morning.

Nutritional Value (per serving):

Calories: 250 kcal

Protein: 10g

Fat: 4g
 Carbohydrates: 42g
